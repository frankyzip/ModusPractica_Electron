## üìñ Understanding AGPL-3.0

AGPL-3.0 is more protective than traditional open source licenses:

**Traditional GPL:**

```
Client ‚Üí Your App ‚Üí Covered
         (If distributed, must share source)
```

**AGPL-3.0:**

```
Client ‚Üí Web Browser ‚Üí Your Server
         (Network use triggers requirement to share source)
```

This means if you provide ModusPractica as a service to users over a network (SaaS), you must make your modified source available to users.

**Example scenarios:**

‚úÖ **AGPL OK:** You modify ModusPractica for internal use
